MEATPLANT (now Masoklove) is "project" that I started in 2021 as a way to express through music my frustration with life and clinical depression. However, I want MEATPLANT to be more than that. I'm not entirely sure what, and I don't think I could define it beyond "a project for some of my art". What kind of art? mostly music and pictures. maybe a short story here or there and some zines. MEATPLANT is more of an undefined theme for myself more than anything.
Not everything on this website is explicitly MEATPLANT related. Some of it is just stuff I make that I want to put somewhere for others to see for whatever reason.
There is also some fun hidden stuff on this site. I won't elaborate further
